races

The 10K will start above the Oakley Reservoir as usual. The distance is a GPS measured 10K. The course follows a scenic, winding road from above the dam, across the Goose Creek drainage and into town, ending at the Oakley City Park.

The 5K course is the last half of the 10K course. The start will be a little southwest of where the Goose Creek road forks left to Goose Creek and right to the dam.

The one mile kid's race around town will be held at approximately 8:30am for children under 12 years of age. Check in by the Oakley City Office at 8:00am. A $3.00 fee and a signed waiver are required.

Registration

Registration must be completed online. Early arrivers may pick up race packets or register Friday, July 19, at the City Park between the hours of 5pm and 7pm.

10K Entry Fee: $40 (non-refundable)

Youth 17 and under will receive a $5 discount.

5K Entry Fee: $35 (non-refundable)

Youth 17 and under will receive a $5 discount.

Prices increase June 16th.

Prizes

All 10K and 5K participants will receive:

A free breakfast. Your bib number is your breakfast ticket. If you prefer not to eat breakfast, another family member may use your bib number.

A deluxe 2024 T-shirt.

An awards ceremony will take place at the completion of the races. Prizes will be awarded to the top three 10K and 5K overall male and female winners.
