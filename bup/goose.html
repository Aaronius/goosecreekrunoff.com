10K RACE

The race starts at the Oakley City Park, making a loop around historic Oakley and finishing with a scenic out-and-back through the Goose Creek area west of town. The race will end in the park where activities for the Pioneer Day Celebration will be in progress.

Click here for a map of the race

FUN WALK

A 4-mile fun walk will also be held this year. The same entry fee, check-in/start times apply. The course will be the same as the 10K, without the out-and-back. No official time will be taken and no prizes awarded, but door prizes will be given away.

KIDS RACE

A 1-mile kids race around town will be held at approximately 8:00 AM for children under 12 years of age. Check-in by the Oakley City Office at 7:30 AM. A $2.00 fee and signed waiver are required.
